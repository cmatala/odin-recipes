Pasta Recipe

Description

This delicious pasta recipe features a rich tomato sauce, complemented by a blend of Italian herbs and fresh vegetables. It's a classic and comforting dish that is quick and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 250g of pasta
  • 1 can (400g) of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, saut√© until softened.
  3. Add the bell pepper and zucchini to the skillet. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and add dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Combine the cooked pasta with the sauce. Toss until pasta is well coated.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.
