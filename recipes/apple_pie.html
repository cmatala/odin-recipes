Apple Pie

Description

This classic apple pie recipe features a flaky crust and a delicious apple filling. It's the perfect dessert for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups sliced and peeled apples
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 package refrigerated pie crusts
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • Sugar (for sprinkling)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine sliced apples, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, lemon juice, and flour. Toss until apples are evenly coated.
  3. Roll out one pie crust and place it in a 9-inch pie dish. Pour in the apple filling.
  4. Roll out the second pie crust and place it over the filling. Trim and crimp the edges to seal the pie.
  5. Brush the top crust with beaten egg and sprinkle with sugar.
  6. Make several small slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  7. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until the crust is golden brown. Let it cool before serving.
